Services

What I bring to your engineering and BIM projects.

Structural Design

Analysis and design of concrete and steel structures including load path evaluation, structural behavior assessment, and performance-based design following international standards.

Seismic & Dynamic Analysis

Structural and seismic studies using ETABS for dynamic building analysis, including seismic response evaluation, modal analysis, and performance requirements.

BIM Coordination

Professional Revit modeling with BIM coordination, LOD workflow management, and multidisciplinary information quality assurance for architectural and structural teams.

Parametric Design

Grasshopper-based parametric workflows for architectural form exploration, structural optimization, weight efficiency analysis, and utilization ratio control.

Technical Documentation

Clear, coordinated technical documentation aligned with project standards — drawings, reports, model outputs, and execution follow-up for construction teams.

Foundation & Slab Design

Using SAFE for slab and foundation analysis, and SAP2000 for specialized elements such as water tanks, stairs, and retaining structures.
